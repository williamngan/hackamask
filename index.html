Hack-a-MaskALPHA

Hack-a-Mask is a weekend hacking project. Our goal is to design a mask that:  
▸ uses only household materials
▸ requires no special equipments to make
▸ can be made in 1-2 minutes

Inspired by concepts in origami, we created Hack-a-Mask. It takes only 8 folds to make, and provides a good fit around the face.

Although we don't know the effectiveness of Hack-a-Mask, there are evidences ( one, two, three ) to indicate that when you wear a mask, you protects others.

Mass adoption of masks by communities may help us flatten the curve of this coronavirus pandemic.
